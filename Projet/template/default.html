Welcome to Fithnity the ESPRIT carpooling app!

Fithnity About

Fithnity is a carpooling app designed for ESPRIT students, enabling them to share daily commutes in an affordable and eco-friendly way. It connects drivers and passengers through a secure and convenient platform.

Our Vision

Sustainable Mobility, Student Connection

Our Mision

Promote Sustainability, Simplify Commuting.

Join Fithnity for a secure, affordable, and eco-friendly carpooling experience. Verified profiles, transparent ratings, and a user-friendly platform ensure a safe and enjoyable journey for ESPRIT students.
